Pondicherry

Pondicherry is the French colonial settlement in Tamil Nadu, India, which is now a Union Territory. The place is famous for the French towns, beaches and cheap rates of commodities like petrol.

Pichavaram is the second largest mangrove and boating through these mangroves is an experience you never want to miss. Boating offered by the forest department is recommended. Next, visit the Chithambaram Temple - the splendid architecture and their colourful paintings are going to leave you in awe! Once back to Pondicherry, try the famous Madurai Jigarthanda and walk through the streets.

Catch the sunrise the next day at the Rock Beach and head out to Auroville - Matri Mandir, the stories of their communities, oragnic food offered in their cafes, scented candles and other items that they sell are the things which you can explore here. After lunch, head out to Sacred Heart Basillica, Aurobino Ashram and set out to explore White Town. You could rent Vespa for cheap rate so consider that as petrol prices are also cheap here and view the houses and buildings painted in different colours like Blue, Pink, Yellow, White etc. The long roads along the Rock Beach will let you see the Gandhi Statue, Lighthouse and other things. View the beautiful streets, eat at the cafes, do some shopping and end your day!
